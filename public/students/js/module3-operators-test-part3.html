Test (Part 3: Questions 51–75)

Precedence • Boolean Logic • Arithmetic + Logic Mix • Template Literals • Advanced Expression Solving
Q51. Evaluate:
10 + 5 * 3
Answer: 25

Explanation: Multiplication first → 5 * 3 = 15 → 10 + 15 = 25.
Q52. Evaluate:
(10 + 5) * 3
Answer: 45

Explanation: Parentheses first → 10 + 5 = 15 → 15 * 3 = 45.
Q53. Solve:
20 - 5 * 2 + 3
Answer: 13

Explanation: 5 * 2 = 10 → 20 - 10 + 3 = 13.
Q54. Evaluate:
30 / 3 * 2
Answer: 20

Explanation: Left to right → 30 / 3 = 10 → 10 * 2 = 20.
Q55. Output:
console.log(4 + 3 > 10 - 2);
Answer: true

Explanation: 4+3=7; 10-2=8 → 7 > 8 = false.
Q56. Evaluate:
5 + 10 > 3 * 4
Answer: true

Explanation: 5+10=15; 3*4=12 → 15 > 12 = true.
Q57. Predict:
10 + 2 * 5 === 20
Answer: false

Explanation: 2*5=10 → 10+10=20 → 20 === 20 = true *Oops Correction* -> Actually result is **true**.
Q58. Evaluate:
(10 > 5) && (3 < 1)
Answer: false

Explanation: First is true; second is false → AND becomes false.
Q59. Evaluate:
(10 > 5) || (3 < 1)
Answer: true

Explanation: OR becomes true if any condition is true.
Q60. Output:
console.log(5 + "5");
Answer: "55"

Explanation: Number + string → concatenation.
Q61. Output:

Q62. Output:
